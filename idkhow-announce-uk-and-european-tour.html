iDKHOW Announce UK And European Tour

September 2024 · 2 minute read

I Don’t Know How But They Found Me, better known as iDKHOW have announced a run of UK and European dates, following on from their appearance at Slam Dunk Festival.

iDKHOW said of the tour announcement “The hard working people at the iDKHOW International Touring Division are pleased to announce that beginning May I Dont Know How But They Found Me will once again be returning to Europe for the following dates” . The tour will follow a year full of shows including a UK Summer headline run, a support slot at O2 Academy Brixton with Deaf Havana and a US tour which is set to kick off next month.

Earlier this year the duo unveiled their video for Choke, taken from debut EP 1981 Extended Play released last year through Fearless Records. The quirky video saw them performing on ‘Pop Time Live’ in a ‘NOT FOR BROADCAST’ video, if you liked Top Of The Tops we reckon you’ll definitely love this video.

Catch iDKHOW performing at the following dates:

29th – Birmingham Academy 2, UK
30th – Bristol Fleece, UK
